  YES → Add "High" to severity candidates, add reason "score_or_kd_impossible"

Step 2: [Decision] Did any match trigger flag_short_match OR flag_extreme_kills?
  YES → Add "Medium" to severity candidates, add reason "short_match_or_extreme_kills"

  YES → Override severity to "High" (repeat-offense escalation)

Step 6: [Decision] Were any candidates found?
  NO → Player is clean, skip
  YES → Output row: player_id | flagged_matches | flag_reasons | severity
